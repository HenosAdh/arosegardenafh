About our home

A real home, run by one family.

A Rose Garden Adult Family Home is a licensed adult family home in Mill Creek, Washington. We provide compassionate, personalized, around-the-clock care in a safe, comfortable, family-like home.

We preserve each resident's dignity, independence, and quality of life while meeting their physical, emotional, and social needs. We run two houses in Mill Creek, and both share the same owners, the same team, and the same standard of care.

"Care that meets your parent's needs, and protects who they are."

Daily life

A calm, full day, every day.

Home-cooked meals

Three fresh meals a day at a shared table, cooked here to suit each resident's diet and the foods they love.

Companionship, day and night

Someone is always nearby for conversation, a little music, or just company, awake and on-site around the clock.

Fresh air and garden time

Time on the deck, in the garden, and short walks, plus family visits, keep the days full and unhurried.

Personal care

Bathing, showering, grooming, dressing, shaving, oral hygiene, and toileting, all handled gently, with dignity and privacy.

Mobility assistance

A steady hand with walking, transferring (for example bed to chair), repositioning, and fall prevention, whenever it is needed.
